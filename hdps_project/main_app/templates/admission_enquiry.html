Admission Procedure

Guidelines and requirements for enrolling your child

General Admission Guidelines

Admissions are open for all classes based on availability and age eligibility. Parents or guardians are requested to follow the procedure outlined below to ensure a smooth enrollment process.

Application Submission

Submit the registration form with the required documents, including recent photographs and academic records. Forms can be collected from the school office or downloaded online.

Eligibility Verification

Students are admitted based on age criteria, prior academic performance, and the completion of necessary prerequisites for the selected class.

Orientation & Guidance

Parents and students are invited to an orientation session to familiarize themselves with school rules, curriculum, and the academic environment.

Fee & Registration

All fees and registration formalities should be completed before the commencement of classes. School administration will provide the detailed schedule and fee structure.

Rules & Dress Code

Student Conduct

Students are expected to respect teachers, peers, and school property. Participation in school activities, maintaining discipline, and ethical behavior is mandatory.

Dress Code

Students are required to wear the prescribed uniform during school hours. Uniforms should be neat, clean, and appropriate for both regular days and special occasions. Guidelines for winter and sports attire will be provided separately.

Guidelines for Guardians

  • Ensure timely attendance and encourage regular participation in academic and extracurricular activities.
  • Monitor your child’s progress and provide guidance at home.
  • All school payments should be made as per the schedule.
  • Support your child in following school rules, maintaining discipline, and respecting peers and staff.
  • Communicate with school authorities regarding concerns or suggestions for your child’s growth.

Student Commitment & Values

  • Show respect and courtesy to parents, teachers, and peers.
  • Strive for honesty, integrity, and responsibility in all actions.
  • Participate actively in school activities, promoting teamwork, cooperation, and service to the community.
  • Pursue academic excellence while balancing personal growth and ethical behavior.
